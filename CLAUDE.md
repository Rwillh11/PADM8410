This folder contains slides, lecture materials, data and problem sets for PADM 8410.

This is my public data analysis for MPA students class. They are learning R (tidyverse) and the fundamentals of data analysis. They are, in general, non-technical and haven't seen much math since high school. Because of this, examples should be policy related, intuitive and err on the side of overexplaining.

When introducting a concept with R, use tidyverse and explain step by step.
